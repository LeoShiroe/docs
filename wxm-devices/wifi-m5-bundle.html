WiFi M5 Bundle with Weather Station

This is our developer friendly, open source, local weather data miner bundled with an all-in-one weather station.

Its setup is very straightforward with it being summarized in the following general steps:

  1. Find the best possible place in your area according to the criteria (see Good practice guides for placing the outdoor sensor).
  2. Install the outdoor sensor on a metallic mast far from obstacles.
  3. Activate both the outdoor sensor and the data miner (WXM-M5).
  4. Connect WXM-M5 to the internet.
  5. Get your data on the screen of your phone.

See below for a short description of the individual parts of this weather station, along with a setup guide for the M5 and the weather station.
